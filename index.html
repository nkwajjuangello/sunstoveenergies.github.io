    About Us

    SUNSTOVE ENERGIES is a green energy startup operating in the Central region of Uganda.

    Our Mission

    To create sustainable solutions to environmental degradation and climate change problems with use of electrical and electronic technologies.

    Our Plan

    To switch 500,000 customers (households, schools, hotels, prisons and refugee camps) from carbon-based cooking to eco-friendly sustainable solar powered cooking by 2050.

    Our Vision

    To create research and innovation hub that uses modern technology to build sustainable solutions to climate and environmental challenges.

    OUR PRODUCTS AND SERVICES
